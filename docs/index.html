About

RainCharts shows you the probability of rain for the next hour and the next day using easy-to-read visual charts. The app uses your location to provide accurate local forecasts, so you always know when to grab an umbrella.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the app need my location?

RainCharts uses your location to fetch accurate rain forecasts for your area. Without location access, we can't show you local weather data.

The app shows "No location available"

Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services and ensure RainCharts has permission to access your location.

How often does the forecast update?

The forecast updates automatically when you open the app or when your location changes. You can also pull down to refresh manually.

Privacy

RainCharts uses your location solely to provide local rain forecasts. Your location data is sent to our forecast service to retrieve weather data but is not stored or shared with third parties.

We do not collect any personal information, and there are no analytics or tracking in the app.
